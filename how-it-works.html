Getting Started

Begin your learning journey

Welcome to UniLingo! We're excited to help you master academic English. Getting started is simple and takes just a few minutes.

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email and create a secure password. We'll send you a confirmation link to verify your account.

Personalize Your Experience

Tell us about yourself during setup:

  • Your native language and target language
  • Your field of study (Engineering, Business, Medicine, Law, etc.)
  • Your current proficiency level
  • How much time you can dedicate daily
  • Your specific learning goals

Smart Placement Assessment

UniLingo uses smart technology to assess your current level. This isn't a test you need to study for - we simply want to understand where you're starting from so we can create the perfect learning path for you.

Pro Tip

Be honest about your goals and available time. This helps us create the most effective personalized learning path for your needs.

CEFR Lessons

Learn at every level

UniLingo offers structured lessons organized by CEFR levels from beginner (A1) to advanced (C2).

Lesson Types

Words Lessons

Build vocabulary with clear definitions, example sentences, and audio pronunciation from native speakers.

Listen Lessons

Match words to sounds, complete comprehension exercises, and adjust playback speed to challenge yourself.

Write Lessons

Construct sentences with new vocabulary through fill-in-the-blank exercises and short writing prompts with instant feedback.

Speak Lessons

Practice pronunciation with AI-powered speech recognition. Get scores, feedback, and personalized tips to improve.

Roleplay Lessons

Practice realistic conversations with our AI avatar that responds naturally and helps you build confidence.

Seamless Experience

Every lesson supports resume functionality. Pick up exactly where you left off, anytime, anywhere. Your progress is automatically saved across all devices.

Personal Lessons

Create Your Own Custom Content

Create your own lessons using the Personal Lessons feature: upload PDFs, images (textbooks, lecture slides), or photos and the app will extract subject-specific vocabulary and generate a tailored course.

How It Works

  • Upload PDFs from textbooks or lecture slides
  • Upload images or photos of content
  • AI extracts subject-specific vocabulary
  • Generates a complete tailored course
  • Alternatively, write your own lessons from scratch

Built-in Exercises

The system creates a complete curriculum with all exercise types:

  • Words - Learn vocabulary from your materials
  • Listen - Practice with audio exercises
  • Write - Complete writing exercises
  • Speak - Practice pronunciation
  • Roleplay - Conversational scenarios

Personalized Curriculum

  • Full difficulty control - set your own pace
  • Focus on specific topics you need
  • Track progress separately for each lesson set
  • Organize sets like "Midterm Prep" or "Chapter 5 Engineering Terms"

Key Benefit

This feature gives you a personalized curriculum alongside the standard one. Learn exactly what you need for your courses while still benefiting from the structured CEFR lessons.

Smart Flashcards

Master vocabulary faster

UniLingo's flashcard system adapts to your needs and helps you remember vocabulary for the long term using scientifically proven learning methods.

Create Custom Flashcards

Build your personal vocabulary library:

  • Upload images to make cards more memorable
  • Add terms in both languages
  • Organize by topic or lesson
  • Connect words to real experiences

Spaced Repetition Technology

Our smart system uses spaced repetition - a scientifically proven method that optimizes your memory:

  • Cards you struggle with appear more frequently
  • Words you've mastered are shown less often
  • Reviews scheduled at optimal times for memory

Study Modes

Choose from review mode for casual browsing, quiz mode with scoring, or practice mode for specific difficulty levels.

Interactive Games

Learn through play

Learning through games makes studying feel like fun. UniLingo offers eight different interactive games to reinforce vocabulary and grammar.

Our Games

Flashcard Quiz - Multiple-choice questions based on your flashcard collection

Memory Match - Match vocabulary words with their definitions

Word Scramble - Unscramble letters to form correct words

Hangman - Classic word guessing with academic vocabulary

Sentence Scramble - Rearrange words to form correct sentences

Speed Challenge - Race against the clock to test knowledge

Gravity Game - Catch falling words to form sentences

Type What You Hear - Transcribe audio to improve listening and spelling

Game Features

  • Difficulty selection (Beginner, Intermediate, Expert)
  • Topic filtering
  • Score tracking and personal bests
  • XP rewards

Progress & Analytics

Track your journey

Seeing your improvement is one of the most motivating parts of learning. UniLingo gives you detailed insights into your progress.

Level System

Advance through six levels: Beginner → Elementary → Intermediate → Advanced → Expert → Master

Streak Tracking

Building daily study habits is crucial. Our streak counter shows consecutive days studied and keeps you motivated.

Weekly & Monthly Reports

Receive comprehensive summaries showing:

  • Lessons completed and games played
  • Total study time
  • Achievements unlocked

Achievement Badges

Unlock achievements for milestones like perfect week streaks, 100 words mastered, all games completed, and more.

XP & Rewards System

Gamified learning

UniLingo gamifies your learning with an engaging XP system that rewards every action toward improving your language skills.

Earning Experience Points

You earn XP for completing activities:

  • Completing lessons (varies by difficulty)
  • Playing games and improving scores
  • Reviewing flashcards
  • Finishing daily challenges
  • Maintaining study streaks

Level Progression

As you accumulate XP, you advance through levels with visual progress bars showing your journey from Beginner to Master.

Celebration Animations

Every time you level up, enjoy special celebration animations featuring your AI avatar. These moments recognize your achievement and keep you motivated.

Daily Challenge

Fresh learning every day

Every day brings a new opportunity to learn and improve with your personalized daily challenge.

Random Daily Game

Each day, UniLingo selects a random game as your special challenge. This keeps your learning routine fresh and ensures you practice all game types regularly.

Special Rewards

Completing your daily challenge earns you:

  • Bonus XP (more than regular play)
  • Progress toward maintaining your streak
  • A sense of accomplishment each day

Quick Access

Your dashboard makes it easy to find and start your daily challenge with a single tap. The challenge card is prominently displayed so you'll never miss an opportunity to earn bonus points.

Image Upload & OCR

Learn from your own materials

Learn vocabulary directly from your course materials with UniLingo's powerful image upload and OCR technology.

How It Works

  • Upload photos of documents, books, or notes
  • OCR technology extracts text automatically
  • AI identifies key vocabulary from your materials
  • Instantly creates flashcards from your course content

Perfect For

Extract vocabulary from textbooks, lecture notes, academic papers, or any document. Learn the exact terminology used in your field of study.

Pro Tip

Take photos of your actual course materials and UniLingo will automatically create personalized flashcards with the vocabulary you need to master.

Audio Lessons

Learn on the go

Never stop learning with UniLingo's audio lessons and podcasts. Perfect for busy students who want to maximize their learning time.

Audio Features

  • High-quality native speaker pronunciation
  • Adjustable playback speed
  • Background play mode for multitasking
  • Comprehension exercises after listening

Perfect For

Listen during commutes, workouts, or any time you're on the go. Learning doesn't stop when you're away from your desk.

Offline Capability

Download audio content to learn without internet access. Perfect for flights, commutes, or areas with poor connectivity.

The Arcade

Your Rewards Come to Life

The Arcade is a special place where your hard-earned XP transforms into visible rewards and personalized experiences.

What Can You Buy?

  • Cosmetic items for your avatar
  • Custom backgrounds and themes
  • Bonus content and features
  • Achievement showcases
  • Personalization options

How It Works

Earn XP from completing lessons, playing games, and reviewing flashcards. Then visit the Arcade to spend your points on items that make your UniLingo experience uniquely yours.

Reward Your Progress

  • Reach a new level? Spend XP on a special item
  • Complete a daily challenge? Treat yourself to a new background
  • Build a long streak? Unlock rare cosmetic rewards

Key Benefit

The Arcade ties visible progress to daily study habits. When you can see and showcase your achievements, staying motivated becomes much easier. It turns learning into a game where rewards come to life.

Avatar

Your Personalized Guide

Create and personalize your own avatar that guides you through lessons, provides encouragement, and celebrates your successes.

Design Your Avatar

  • Customize appearance and style
  • Choose outfits and accessories
  • Select facial features and expressions
  • Personalize colors and themes

In-Game Guidance

Your avatar appears throughout your learning journey:

  • Animated guidance during lessons
  • Celebrations when you succeed
  • Encouragement when you struggle
  • Motivational messages and tips

Milestone Celebrations

Watch your avatar celebrate your achievements. When you reach a new level, complete a difficult lesson, or maintain a long streak, your avatar joins in the celebration with special animations and messages.

Why It Matters

Your avatar adds a friendlier, more engaging layer to practice. Instead of learning alone, you have a personalized guide cheering you on, making motivation easier and learning more enjoyable.
